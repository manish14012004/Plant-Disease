NOTE*

1.REI (Restricted Entry Interval): REI refers to the minimum amount of time that must pass after a pesticide or chemical has been applied to a crop before it is safe for workers to re-enter the treated area without protective gear.

2.PHI (Pre-Harvest Interval): PHI refers to the minimum number of days that must pass after a pesticide or chemical has been applied before the crop can be harvested. This ensures that pesticide residues degrade to acceptable levels before the crop is consumed.
